Innerve, in collaboration with Coding Ninjas, proudly presents Innerve Hacks, the largest pan-India Hackathon. This event offers a remarkable platform for young innovators to champion the cause of inclusivity. Participants are tasked with creating a work model or a promising prototype of their shortlisted idea within the given time frame, all with the goal of fostering a more inclusive world. Join us on this journey to drive positive change through innovation and Gender diversity.

ROUNDS


This will be a coding contest on Coding Ninjas! All registered teams will participate in this round. All the members of a team will attempt the technical test and an average of the team will be calculated.


It will be a 60 hours development period with the teams' respective mentors. Post which, the teams will have a 12 hour period to submit their presentations (GitHub repos included in pdf) on Unstop. The Round Evaluation will be based on the mentors' feedback and the progress of the Team.


Final Presentation Round at IGDTUW Campus, Where the teams will present their solution project offline to the panel of esteemed judges.
